About this blog

Partly, I made this website because I wanted to review metal music. But mainly, I needed to make a website for my digital technology assessment and I wanted to do something other than make an ad for my school. This felt natural to me, because it gave me plenty of freedom to think for myself nad incorporate cool ideas, while still having a defined scope of what I needed to work on. After all, you can do a lot of cool stuff with websites, but you can do a lot less when you only have 4/5 weeks to do it. But hey: metal is fun!
